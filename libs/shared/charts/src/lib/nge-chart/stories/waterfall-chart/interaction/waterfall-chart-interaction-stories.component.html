Waterfall Chart - Interactive Controls

Use the Storybook controls panel to adjust the waterfall configuration in real-time. Toggle cumulative for the running-total % overlay, connectors for the step lines, and showTooltip for hover details. Press Randomize to re-roll the movements.

Available Controls

  • Margins: chart margins (leave room on the right for the % axis)
  • Layout: bar padding and corner radius
  • Visibility: value labels, step connectors, cumulative overlay
  • Tooltip: enable / disable hover tooltips
  • Axis Labels: X and Y axis titles
  • Colors: rise / fall / total bar colors and the cumulative line color
