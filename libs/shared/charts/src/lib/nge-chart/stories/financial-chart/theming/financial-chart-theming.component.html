Financial Chart - Theming Examples

The financial layer reads a theme.financial slice: up / down (candlestick bodies + renko bricks — color, fillOpacity, stroke, strokeWidth), wick (candlestick high–low line + kagi connector), and kagi (the yang / yin line colors and widths). Up defaults to green, down to red; override any of them explicitly.

Default Theme

No overrides — up candles fill green (#4caf50), down candles red (#f44336), and the wick reads the muted --chart-on-surface-variant.

Custom Up / Down Colors (theme.financial.up / down)

Recolor both bodies — a teal up candle and an amber down candle, each with a matching outline and a slate wick — for a fully bespoke candlestick palette.

Hollow Candlestick (up.fillOpacity: 0)

The classic hollow look: up candles drop their fill (fillOpacity: 0, outline only) while down candles stay solid, so a session's direction reads from whether its body is filled.

Recolored Kagi (theme.financial.kagi)

In variant: 'kagi' the yang (thick) / yin (thin) segments take their color and width from the kagi slot — here an indigo yang widened against a thin orange yin.

Same Data, Three Variants
