Sparkline Chart - Theming Examples

Sparklines resolve their colours from the ambient --chart-* tokens, so the same config re-themes just by overriding those tokens on a wrapping element. Per-mark colours can also be pinned directly through preset options (lastValueColor, winColor / lossColor / tieColor).
